Quick Reference — Slope Rating Guide

What Slope Ratings Mean

Slope RatingCourse DescriptionTypical Example
113Standard — average difficultyMost parkland club courses
120–130Moderately challengingQuality heathland / links
130–140Significantly challengingChampionship layouts
140–155Severe — expert requiredDifficult links, mountain courses

A slope rating above 130 means the course penalises higher handicaps significantly — your playing handicap will be meaningfully higher than your index. A slope below 113 means the course plays easier than standard for all handicaps.
